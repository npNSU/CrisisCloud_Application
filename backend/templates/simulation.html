Welcome to the Hurricane Irene simulation
This is a walkthrough of CrisisCloud built around Hurricane Irene, which hit Hampton Roads on August 27, 2011. Everything you see here is based on what actually happened that day. The clock at the top is running on your real current time so it always feels like the storm is happening right now, no matter when you open this page.

Work through each step using the buttons above or the arrows at the bottom of each card. You can jump to any step at any time. When you are ready to go back to the real operational dashboard, just click Live Dashboard in the nav above.
Irene brought 4 to 8 feet of storm surge into Hampton Roads, sustained winds between 70 and 80 mph, widespread power outages across Norfolk, Chesapeake, Portsmouth and Virginia Beach, and up to 12 inches of rain across eastern Virginia. It was one of the most disruptive storms the region had seen since Floyd in 1999.
This simulation does not replace the live dashboard. It runs alongside it so your team can learn the tools before they need them in a real emergency.

The live emergency map
This is a real interactive map centered on Hampton Roads. You can drag it around, pinch or scroll to zoom in and out, and tap any marker to see what is going on at that location. Use the city filter to narrow down what you see, or tap Use my location to center the map on where you are right now.
The S markers are open shelters. The F markers show active flooding zones reported during Irene. The red triangles are incidents like downed trees and road closures. Tap any of them for more details.

Submitting a field report
Use the search bar built into the map to find the address where you are reporting from. Start typing something like Shore Drive Norfolk or 700 Park and a list of matching addresses will appear. Pick the right one and a pin will drop on the map at that exact spot. You can also tap Use my location to skip the search and use your GPS instead.
The address search is focused on Hampton Roads so local streets come up first. Once you pick an address or use your location, fill in the form below and hit submit.
