Step 1 of 7
Welcome to the Hurricane Irene simulation
This tutorial walks you through every feature of CrisisCloud using a live re-creation of Hurricane Irene (August 27, 2011). The clock runs on your real current EST time so it always feels active.
During Irene, Hampton Roads experienced storm surge of 4–8 ft, sustained winds of 70–80 mph, widespread power outages, and up to 12 inches of rainfall.
The full live dashboard is always one click away at the Live Dashboard tab above.
